Recipe #1 - Chicken Noodle

  • Time: 45 mins
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken drumsticks 2
Noodle 0.5 kg
Chicken Sauce 4 teaspoon

Step 1: Seasoned the chicken drumsticks with salt and pepper.
Deep fried the chicken drumstick till the skin turned golden.

Step 2: Cook the noodle in boiling water for 5 mins.
Pour away the hot water. Add 2 teaspoons of soy sauce and mix
thoroughly with the noodle.

Step 3: Transfer the noodle to your favourite ceramic bowl.
Place the fried chicken drumsticks on top of the noodles.
Sprinkle some corriander and seasame seeds.

Source: Grandma's Chicken Noodle
